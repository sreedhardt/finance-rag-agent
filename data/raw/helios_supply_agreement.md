# Amended and Restated Wafer Supply Agreement

**Between:** Orion Semiconductor, Inc. ("Buyer") and Helios Foundry Ltd.
("Supplier"). **Effective:** first day of Buyer's fiscal year 2025.

## 1. Supply and Capacity Commitment

Supplier shall reserve for Buyer a wafer fabrication capacity of no less than
34,000 wafer starts per quarter on Supplier's N3E process node. Buyer's
committed minimum annual purchase volume shall be one hundred twenty thousand
(120,000) wafers, corresponding to a **minimum annual purchase commitment of
USD 1,200 million for fiscal year 2025** and USD 1,450 million for fiscal year
2026.

## 2. Pricing and Indexation

The baseline price shall be **USD 9,400 per wafer** for the N3E node. Prices
may be adjusted quarterly to reflect documented changes in substrate, chemical,
and energy input costs, provided that no single quarterly adjustment shall
exceed **3%** of the then-current price, and cumulative adjustments within a
fiscal year shall not exceed 8%.

## 3. Payment Terms

Supplier shall invoice Buyer upon wafer-out. Payment is due **net forty-five
(45) days** from receipt of a correct invoice. Late payments accrue interest at
1% per month. Buyer may withhold amounts subject to a good-faith yield dispute,
not to exceed 10% of the invoiced amount.

## 4. Yield and Quality

Supplier warrants a minimum average yield of 88% measured across each rolling
quarter. If yield falls below 84% for two consecutive quarters, Buyer may
reduce its committed volume for the following fiscal year by up to 20% without
penalty.

## 5. Shortfall and Excess

If Buyer's actual purchases in a fiscal year fall below the minimum annual
purchase commitment, Buyer shall pay Supplier a shortfall fee equal to 55% of
the difference between the commitment and actual purchases, except where the
shortfall results from Supplier's failure to deliver reserved capacity.

## 6. Term and Termination

The agreement runs through the end of Buyer's fiscal year 2027. Either party
may terminate for convenience on **one hundred eighty (180) days'** written
notice, provided that termination by Buyer does not extinguish the current
fiscal year's minimum purchase commitment. Either party may terminate for
material breach uncured within 45 days of notice.

## 7. Limitation of Liability

Except for breaches of confidentiality or IP indemnification obligations,
each party's aggregate liability under this agreement is capped at **the fees
paid or payable by Buyer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim**.
Neither party is liable for consequential, incidental, or punitive damages.

## 8. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.
